Discover your signature scent with Airomas

  1. Go to Discover: Click on the Discover tab to start finding fragrances.
  2. Fill in your preferences: Enter your age, gender, occupation, mood, activities, style, season, fragrance type, and max price.
  3. Submit your form: Click Find My Scent and let Airomas suggest fragrances that match your style.
  4. Explore scents: Review the recommended perfumes, see their notes, prices, and official links.
  5. Chat for help: Use the Chat tab to ask questions or get more personalized guidance.
